Zhou Yuguang (周宇光)

About Me

I am a Ph.D student in HKUST, advised by Prof. Shuai Wang. Before this, I obtained my bachelor's degree at Beihang University and conducted research on AI adversarial attacks under the supervision of Prof. Aishan Liu. I earned my master's degree at National University of Singapore, where I studied data protection and privacy issues of large language models with Prof. Jiaheng Zhang. I also interned at ByteDance Singapore. My research interest mainly lies in LLM-based agent safety, foundation model data privacy and large model interpretability.
